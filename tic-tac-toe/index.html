Instructions

Tic-Tac-Toe: Two players, X and O, take turns marking spaces in a 3x3 grid.

The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row wins.

In this version, you play as X against AI as O. Tap cells to play.

Use buttons for new game, hint (highlight suggested move), check status, auto-solve (AI plays both sides to finish), and instructions.
