Yahtzee Game Rules

  • Roll dice up to 3 times per turn to achieve the best combination.
  • Choose which dice to keep between rolls.
  • After rolling, select a category to score your roll.
  • Each category can only be used once per game.
  • Upper section: Score the sum of specific numbers.
  • Lower section: Score special combinations like Full House, Straights, etc.
  • Bonus: Score 35 bonus points if your upper section total is 63 or more.
  • Yahtzee: Score 50 points for five of a kind.
  • The game ends after 13 rounds, and the player with the highest total wins!
