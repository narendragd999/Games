HOW TO PLAY

Nonograms are picture logic puzzles where you fill in cells to reveal a hidden picture.

Rules:

  • Numbers on the sides indicate groups of consecutive filled cells
  • "2 1" means there's a group of 2 filled cells, then at least one empty cell, then 1 filled cell
  • Empty cells can be marked with an X or crossed out
  • Use logic to determine which cells must be filled and which must be empty

Controls:

  • FILL: Fill a cell (left click/tap)
  • MARK: Mark with X (right click/long tap)
  • CROSS: Cross out empty cells
  • Switch between modes using the buttons above the grid

Tips:

  • Start with rows/columns that have large numbers or many groups
  • Use the "Check Solution" button to verify your progress
  • Use hints when you're stuck (limited to 3 per puzzle)
  • The "Auto-Solve" button will solve the puzzle step by step
