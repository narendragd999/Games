How to Play

Balance chemical equations by adjusting coefficients:

  • Change numbers in front of compounds
  • Same atoms must appear on both sides
  • Tap "Check" to verify your solution
  • Use "Auto Solve" for help (reduces points)
  • Complete puzzles before time runs out

Examples:

H₂ + O₂ → H₂O becomes 2H₂ + O₂ → 2H₂O

CH₄ + O₂ → CO₂ + H₂O becomes CH₄ + 2O₂ → CO₂ + 2H₂O
