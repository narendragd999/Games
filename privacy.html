Privacy Policy for Brainsta

Last updated: September 2026

Brainsta (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and safeguarded when you use the Brainsta mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

a) Personal Information

When you sign up or log in to Brainsta, we collect:

  • Email address (used for authentication)
  • User ID generated by Firebase Authentication

We do not collect names, phone numbers, addresses, or payment information.

b) Usage Information

We may store non-sensitive usage data such as:

  • Games viewed or played
  • Likes, favorites, and comments
  • App interaction data for personalization

2. How We Use Your Information

Information collected is used only to:

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Provide personalized game recommendations
  • Save user preferences and interactions
  • Maintain app security and stability

3. Data Storage and Security

Brainsta uses Google Firebase services for authentication and data storage. All data is transmitted securely using encryption (HTTPS).

4. Third-Party Services

Brainsta uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase Authentication
  • Google Firestore Database

These services may collect limited technical data as described in their privacy policy:
https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

5. Ads and Monetization

Brainsta currently does not display advertisements and does not use AdMob or AdSense.

6. WebView and External Content

Brainsta displays HTML5 games hosted on external platforms using WebView. Brainsta does not collect personal data from these external pages and does not access user credentials on those sites.

7. Children’s Privacy

Brainsta is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Data Deletion

Users may request deletion of their account and associated data by contacting us via email. All data will be permanently deleted upon request.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
