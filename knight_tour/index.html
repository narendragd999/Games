How to Play Knight's Tour

The knight moves in an L-shape: 2 squares in one direction, then 1 square perpendicular.

  • Goal: Visit every square on the board exactly once.
  • Tap on highlighted squares to move the knight.
  • You cannot revisit a square you've already been to.
  • Start from any square - we'll place the knight randomly.
  • Complete the tour by visiting all squares!

Pro Tip: Use Warnsdorff's rule: move to the square with the fewest onward moves.
