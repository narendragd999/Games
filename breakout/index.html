HOW TO PLAY

  • Use your mouse or touch to move the paddle left and right
  • On desktop, you can also use ← and → arrow keys
  • Break all the bricks with the ball to complete the level
  • Don't let the ball fall below the paddle or you'll lose a life
  • Some bricks may require multiple hits to break
  • Clear all bricks to advance to the next level

POWER-UPS

  • Green bricks: 1 hit to break
  • Orange bricks: 2 hits to break
  • Red bricks: 3 hits to break
  • Purple bricks: Special - gives extra points
