How to Play Nurikabe

Goal: Create islands of white cells around numbered cells, with black cells forming a continuous sea.

Rules:

  • Each numbered cell belongs to a white island of that exact size
  • Islands cannot touch horizontally or vertically (only diagonally is OK)
  • All black cells must be connected horizontally or vertically
  • No 2√ó2 blocks of black cells are allowed

Controls:

  • Tap once: Mark as black
  • Tap twice: Mark as white
  • Tap three times: Clear marking
