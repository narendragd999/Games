How to Play

Welcome to Lock Picker! Your goal is to crack the lock by matching the target combination.

Rules:

  • Match the Target: The yellow box shows the combination you need to achieve
  • Adjust Your Combination: The blue box shows your current progress
  • Two Ways to Adjust: Click any blue digit above OR click a tumbler below to cycle through values (0-9)
  • Complete the Lock: All digits must match the target to unlock
  • Green Indicators: Correct digits turn green in your combination

What Makes This Challenging?

  • Memory Challenge: Remember the target numbers while adjusting each tumbler
  • Time Pressure: Can you solve it quickly? Try to beat your time!
  • Difficulty Scaling: Easy (3 digits), Medium (6 digits), Hard (9 digits) - more tumblers = harder!

Features:

  • Check Solution: Verify if your combination is correct
  • Hint: Reveals one correct tumbler position
  • Auto-Solve: Automatically solves the puzzle for you
  • New Puzzle: Generate a new lock to crack
  • Progress Bar: Visual feedback showing how many digits are correct

Tips to Score Higher:

  • Take a mental snapshot of the target before adjusting

  • Use hints sparingly - only when truly stuck
